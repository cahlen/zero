## Philosophy

### Open Source Forever
Zero will always be 100% open source under the MIT license. No "open core" tricks. No premium features. No enterprise editions. **Everything is free, forever.**

### Privacy First
Your devices, your data. Zero never phones home. Updates come from *your* GitHub repo (or fork). AI keys stay on *your* devices. There is no Zero cloud.

### Modular by Design
Don't need AI? Remove it. Want to add mesh networking? Add it. Building a fleet of digital picture frames? Use only the display module. **Take what you need, leave what you don't.**

### Community Driven
The best ideas will come from you. We're building the foundation ‚Äî you'll build the future. PRs welcome. Forks encouraged. Build something amazing.
